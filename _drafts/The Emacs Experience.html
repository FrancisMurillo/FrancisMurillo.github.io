Introduction

Being in a company with no other known Emacs user, I write this article as a way for me to express what unspoken awe I feel about Emacs. My goal is not shove people into the gauntlet of Emacs(or vi), I want to be understood. Or maybe just to tell a good story.

What Is Emacs?

My first impression of Emacs is that it is an customizable text editor. The pitch doesn't sound epic and there are other customizable text editors like Notepad++ or Sublime Text with their extensions. What makes it different really different is that it comes with it's own programming language, [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emacs_Lisp%5D%5BElisp](a variant of Lisp)
